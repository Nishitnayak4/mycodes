About Taarangan 2025

What is Taarangan?

The eagerly awaited Annual Inter-Collegiate Cultural Festival of Thakur College of Science and Commerce (Autonomous) — now in its 13th year! This year, we embrace the Marvel Multiverse theme, bringing heroes from every realm and timeline together for an unforgettable experience.

Theme: Multiverse Ka Mahanayak

Legends rise, heroes converge, and realms collide in a spectacle of power, courage, and imagination. Join us on 18th - 20th December 2025 for a celebration like no other!

Why Join?

Experience electrifying performances, thrilling competitions, workshops, and celebrity appearances. Unleash your inner hero and be part of the multiverse madness!

About Thakur College

Thakur Educational Trust (TET) established Thakur College of Science & Commerce in 1992 with a vision to empower students with industry-relevant education. From humble beginnings to a multifaceted institution, TCSC continues to nurture talent and creativity.
